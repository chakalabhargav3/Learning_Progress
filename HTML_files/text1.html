Pet Sitting Instructions

Dear Maria, thank you very much for volunteering to pet-sit for us. In this document you will find detailed care instructions for both pets.

Grizzly

Grizzly is our adorable yellow lab puppy

Please give Grizzly 21/2 cups of his kibble every day. Most importantly, please make sure no matter how much he begs or whines, never feed him extra food.

Acceptable Treats

  • Bacon
  • Chicken Breast
  • Cheese
    • Cheddar
    • Cream Cheese

Princess Popcorn

Princess Popcorn is our goldfish(Carassius auratus). Her tank is an absolute nightmare right now. We have to do something about it. Please follow these cleaning instructions:

  1. Remove Princess Popcorn
  2. Clean the inside glass
  3. Siphon the water
  4. Clean the filter
  5. Return Princess Popcorn to the tank
